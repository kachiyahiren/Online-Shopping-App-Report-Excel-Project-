# Online-Shopping-App-Report(Excel)#

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project is an interactive **Excel-based online shoping app sales dashboard** designed to analyze business performance across multiple dimensions such as time, region, customer demographics, and sales channels.

It enables users to explore sales trends, customer behavior, and operational efficiency using dynamic filters and visualizations.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze monthly sales and order trends
- Compare sales performance across states
- Understand customer demographics (gender & age)
- Evaluate sales contribution by channels
- Track order fulfillment status

---

## 📁 Project File
- `Online Shopping App Report.xlsx` → Contains dataset, pivot tables, and dashboard

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 📌 1. Orders vs Sales Trend
- Monthly comparison of:
  - Total Sales Amount
  - Number of Orders

👉 Insight:
- Peak performance observed around **Feb–Mar**
- Gradual decline in later months → possible seasonality

---

### 📌 2. Sales: Men vs Women
- Women: **64%**
- Men: **36%**

👉 Insight:
- Female customers dominate sales → strong target segment

---

### 📌 3. Order Status Analysis
- Delivered: **92%**
- Cancelled: **3%**
- Returned: **3%**
- Refunded: **2%**

👉 Insight:
- High delivery success rate
- Returns and cancellations are low but still relevant

---

### 📌 4. Top 5 States by Sales
- Maharashtra leads significantly
- Followed by Karnataka, Uttar Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu

👉 Insight:
- Sales are geographically concentrated
- Maharashtra is a key revenue driver

---

### 📌 5. Age vs Gender Analysis
- Adults contribute the highest share
- Women dominate across all age groups

👉 Insight:
- Adult female segment is the most valuable customer group

---

### 📌 6. Sales by Channel
- Amazon (~35%) dominates
- Flipkart and Meesho follow
- Other platforms contribute smaller shares

👉 Insight:
- Marketplace dependency is high → potential platform risk

---

### 🎛 Interactive Filters (Slicers)
- Month
- Channel
- Category (Blouse, Kurta, Saree, etc.)

👉 Enables dynamic analysis across all visuals

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Techniques Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables & Pivot Charts
- Slicers for interactivity
- Data aggregation and segmentation

---

## ⚙️ Data Analysis Approach
1. Data cleaning and structuring
2. Created pivot tables for:
   - Time-based analysis
   - Region-wise sales
   - Category and channel performance
3. Designed interactive dashboard
4. Applied slicers for filtering

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- Sales peak early in the year → seasonal trend
- Women (especially adults) are primary customers
- Maharashtra is the strongest market
- Amazon is the dominant sales channel
- Order fulfillment efficiency is high (92% delivered)
